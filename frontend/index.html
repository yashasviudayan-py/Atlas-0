      About ATLAS-0 What this product is and is not

      ATLAS-0 is a report-first room safety scan: upload a 20-60 second walkthrough and get hazards, evidence frames, confidence signals, recommendations, and a PDF report.

      It is decision support, not a safety certification. The honest product promise is to help people notice fixable room risks faster.

      Fun things to try Daily-use ideas for beta users
      • Run a quick toddler-proofing pass before guests arrive.
      • Try a pet safety scan for loose cords, unstable objects, and climbable surfaces.
      • Do a before-and-after rescan after fixing one room and compare the weekend fix list.
      • Use it as a renter move-in checklist with evidence frames you can keep.
      • Try the daily safety mission when you want a quick, low-pressure reason to scan again.
      • Load the sample report when showing ATLAS-0 to a friend.
      Production readiness lens What operators should watch before opening the beta wider

      ATLAS-0 is strongest when the product loop stays narrow: one room, one report, one fix, one rescan. The operator panels above should stay healthy before inviting more beta users.

      • Upload success rate should stay high before increasing traffic.
      • Report usefulness and wrong/duplicate feedback should be reviewed every beta batch.
      • Object-store artifacts, worker health, and retention cleanup should be checked before hosted demos.
      • Low-confidence findings should remain hidden by default unless users ask for the rawer review mode.
      Warm Trust voice guide How ATLAS-0 should sound across product, PDF, and README

      ATLAS-0 should sound calm, specific, and humble. Prefer “what I saw,” “why it might matter,” and “what to do next” over grand claims or model theater.
